Brick by Brick

A stop-motion short built from LEGO — posed, photographed, and cut together one frame at a time.

Stop motion Hundreds of frames No CGI

What this was

Brick by Brick is a hand-animated stop-motion short built from hundreds of LEGO photographs. Each frame was posed, shot, and sequenced into a short film — frame by frame, with no CGI.

The work is about patience and continuity: tiny movements between stills add up to motion that feels physical because it is. This page will host stills and the final cut when they are ready.
