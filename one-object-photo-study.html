Day Logs

One-Object Photo Study

Photograph the same object every day at the same time — then see what changes when you line the frames up.

Daily capture Flipbook or timelapse Due by Labor Day

The idea

Pick one subject — a fire hydrant, a tree, your desk — and shoot it once per day at a fixed time. The constraint is boring on purpose: same angle, same framing, same clock. The interesting part is what the stack of images shows when summer ends.

The deliverable is either a flipbook or a timelapse built from every frame, due by Labor Day. This page will collect the series as it grows.
